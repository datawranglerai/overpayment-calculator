04 About mortgage overpayments

Making overpayments on your mortgage can significantly reduce the total interest you pay and make you mortgage-free sooner. Even small regular overpayments compound into a big difference over time, because every extra pound goes straight at the principal.

! Check before you overpay

Ask your lender about early repayment charges and overpayment limits. Most fixed-rate deals let you overpay up to 10% of the outstanding balance per year without penalty — above that, charges can apply.

→ How this is calculated

Results assume your interest rate stays the same for the remainder of the term, and that overpayments reduce the term (your regular payment stays level while the extra goes straight at the balance). Interest is applied monthly; most UK lenders accrue daily and charge monthly, which produces very slightly different figures.

  • How much interest you could save over the remaining life of your mortgage
